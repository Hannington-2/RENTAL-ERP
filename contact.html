Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions before you reach out.

What are your support response times?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent technical issues, our priority support ensures a response within 4 hours during business hours.

Do you offer on-site demonstrations?

Yes! We offer both virtual and on-site demonstrations for property managers interested in learning more about our platform. Contact our sales team to schedule.

How can I become a partner?

We're always looking for partnerships with property management companies, real estate agents, and technology providers. Contact our partnership team for more information.

Is there a dedicated account manager?

Yes, our Enterprise and Professional plan customers receive dedicated account managers to help with onboarding, training, and ongoing support.
