Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about registration

Is there a cost to create an account?

Yes! Creating an account is completely free. We offer flexible pricing plans based on your needs, with a free tier available for small property managers.

Can I switch my role after registration?

Yes, you can contact our support team to change your role. For example, if you're a tenant but later become a landlord, we can update your account.

What documents do tenants need to upload?

Tenants may be asked to upload a national ID or passport for verification. This helps ensure secure transactions and compliance.

How long does verification take?

Email verification is instant. If additional document review is needed, it typically takes 1-2 business days.

Need Help Getting Started?

Our support team is here to assist you with any questions about the registration process.
