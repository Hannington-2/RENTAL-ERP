How Our Rental Management Platform Works

Simple, secure, and automated rental management for landlords and tenants. From registration to rent collection in a few easy steps.

Step-by-Step Process

Get started with RentFlow Pro in minutes. Our streamlined process makes rental management effortless.

1

Landlord Registration

Sign up as a landlord or property manager. Verify your email to secure your account and access your dashboard.

  • Create your account in seconds
  • Verify email address
  • Set up your landlord profile
2

Add Properties & Tenants

Add your properties to the platform and invite tenants by entering their name and email address.

  • Add multiple properties
  • Create tenant invitations
  • Set rent amounts and due dates
3

Tenant Verification

Tenants receive a verification email, create their account, and upload required documents.

  • Email verification
  • Document upload
  • Profile completion
4

Automated Payments

Tenants receive rent reminders, make payments via M-Pesa STK Push, and receive instant confirmation.

  • Automated rent reminders
  • One-click M-Pesa payments
  • Instant payment verification
5

Receipts & Reports

Digital receipts are auto-generated for every payment. Landlords can track payments and generate reports.

  • Auto-generated receipts
  • Payment tracking
  • Financial reports

Role-Based Access

Each user gets a tailored dashboard with features specific to their role.

Landlord Dashboard

Manage properties, track payments, view tenant details, and generate financial reports.

Tenant Dashboard

View rent due dates, make payments, download receipts, and communicate with landlords.

Admin Dashboard

Full platform control, user management, system settings, and comprehensive analytics.

Powerful Automation

Let RentFlow Pro handle the repetitive tasks so you can focus on what matters.

  • Automated Rent Reminders
    Tenants receive timely reminders before rent due dates.
  • STK Push Payments
    Secure M-Pesa integration for instant rent collection.
  • Auto-Generated Receipts
    Digital receipts created instantly after every payment.
  • Late Payment Alerts
    Automatic notifications for overdue payments.
