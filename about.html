About RentFlow Pro

Our mission is to simplify rental management for landlords and tenants across Kenya with secure, automated, and modern technology.

Our Story

RentFlow Pro was founded in 2023 with a clear vision: to transform how rental properties are managed across Kenya. We recognized that landlords and tenants were struggling with manual rent collection, paper-based record keeping, and communication challenges.

Our team combined expertise in real estate management and technology to create a comprehensive platform that addresses these pain points. Since launch, we've helped thousands of property managers streamline their operations and provided tenants with a convenient way to manage their rental payments.

Today, RentFlow Pro continues to innovate, adding new features and expanding our reach to serve the growing needs of the rental market in Kenya and beyond.
